About this app arrow_forward

Taptap Send is the Digital Financial Service of Bangladesh.

The Taptap Send app is a fast, secure, and reliable digital financial service app that makes your everyday transactions simple—such as, Send Money (P2P), Mobile Recharge, and more. You’ll receive notifications of your latest activities, and you can easily view your transaction history and summaries.

Taptap Send will soon bring exciting and innovative services to meet customer needs and expectations.

key Features:

Secure Transactions: The Taptap Send app is highly secure and requires your PIN at two different stages for extra safety

Personalization: Add your display name and change your profile picture anytime

Error-Free Money Transfer: Select recipients directly from your contact list to avoid sending money to the wrong number

  • Add Money
  • Send Money (P2P)
  • Mobile Recharge
  • Bill Payments
  • Transaction History
